FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do you really visit homes anywhere in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar?+
Yes. Our doctors cover CIDCO, Garkheda, Osmanpura, Ulkanagari, Samarth Nagar, Jalna Road, Beed Bypass, Waluj, Shahanoorwadi, TV Centre and nearby areas. Message us your locality on WhatsApp and we'll confirm right away.
What happens after I message on WhatsApp?+
Our care team replies quickly, understands your problem, and assigns a dedicated doctor for your condition. The doctor visits your home for the first assessment and explains the full recovery plan — only then do you decide to continue.
Will the same doctor come every time?+
Yes — that's our core promise. One dedicated physiotherapist handles your case from the first assessment to full recovery, so you never repeat your story to a new face.
Do I need any equipment at home?+
No. Your doctor brings everything needed for your sessions — including portable therapy equipment. All you need is a little space and a comfortable spot.
How many sessions will I need?+
It depends on your condition — simple pain relief may take a few sessions, while post-surgery or stroke rehabilitation is a longer journey. Your doctor gives you an honest estimate after the first assessment, and tracks progress at every visit.
Is physiotherapy safe for senior citizens and bedridden patients?+
Absolutely — it's one of our specialities. Our doctors are trained in gentle, senior-friendly techniques for elders, bedridden patients, and post-stroke recovery, with family members guided at every step.
